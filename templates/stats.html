Understanding Your Stats

Game Summary:

  • Games Started: Total number of games you've initiated
  • Games Completed: Games that reached a conclusion (win or loss)
  • Games Abandoned: Games started but not completed

Performance Metrics:

  • Win Rate: Percentage of completed games you've won
  • Wins/Losses: Total count of games won and lost

Scoring Analysis:

  • Avg Winning Score: Your average final score in games you win
  • Avg Losing Score: Your average final score in games you lose
  • Avg Win Margin: Average point difference when you win
  • Avg Loss Margin: Average point difference when you lose

Opponent Analysis:

  • Opponent Score When You Win: Computer's average score in your victories
  • Opponent Score When You Lose: Computer's average score in your defeats
