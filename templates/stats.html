Understanding City Stats

City Aggregation:

  • Unique Players: Number of different IP addresses from your city
  • Games Started: Total games initiated by players from your city
  • Games Completed: Games that reached a conclusion (excludes abandoned games)

Performance Metrics:

  • City Win Rate: Percentage of completed games won by your city
  • Total Wins/Losses: Combined count across all city players

Scoring Analysis:

  • Avg Winning Score: Average final score in games your city wins
  • Avg Losing Score: Average final score in games your city loses
  • Win/Loss Margins: Average point differences in victories and defeats

Time Tracking:

  • First/Last Game: When your city first started playing and most recent activity
  • Opponent Scores: How well Marta performs against your city's players
