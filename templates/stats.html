Statistics Guide ▼
Competitive Stats
Games Started: Total games initiated (including abandoned)
Total Games: Completed games only (excludes abandoned)
Win Rate: Percentage of completed games won
Avg Winning Score: Average final score when you win
Avg Losing Score: Average final score when you lose
Avg Losing Margin: Average points behind when losing
Games Abandoned: Games started but not completed
Detailed Hand Performance
Hands Played: Individual hands with successful bids
Average Bids: Mean tricks bid per hand by you and Marta
Nil Attempts: Times you attempted 0-trick bids
Total Bags: Cumulative penalty points from overtricks
Average Bags: Mean bags per hand (overtricks beyond bid)
Note: Rankings are determined by sports-style competitive performance (win rate, then total wins) rather than hand-level statistics. Competitive stats show game outcomes, while detailed stats show bidding patterns and bag management across individual hands.
